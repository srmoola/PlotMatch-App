PlotMatch

Welcome to the Help page for PlotMatch! If you are here, you probably want to know what the PlotMatch Score is or how to navigate the map

Movement features of the map:
• You can scroll the mousewheel to zoom in and out and you can simply drag on the screen to move the camera.

PlotMatch Score:
• What you basically need to know about the PlotMatch Score is that the closer it is to 100, the better the location is for a business. Our algorithm calculates this number by taking into consideration Noise Pollution, Environmental Effects, Proximity to EV Chargers, and Nearby Traffic.
